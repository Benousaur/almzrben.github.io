About Arcane

Arcane is a visually stunning animated series set in the universe of League of Legends. It explores the delicate balance between the rich city of Piltover and the oppressed underground of Zaun. Follow the origins of iconic champions and uncover the power struggles that shape their destinies.

Meet the Champions

Jinx

The unpredictable and chaotic champion who seeks to spread havoc wherever she goes.

Vi

A fierce enforcer from Zaun, known for her powerful punches and sense of justice.

Jayce

The inventor and protector of Piltover, committed to pushing the boundaries of technology.

Viktor

The visionary scientist whose work is driven by a belief in the power of progress and evolution.

Vander/Warkick

A father figure to many, Vander protects the streets of Zaun with a strong sense of honor.

Heimerdinger

A brilliant scientist and inventor, known for his vast knowledge of technology and innovation.

Caitlyn

The sharp-minded sheriff of Piltover, known for her impeccable skills with a sniper rifle.

Silco

A ruthless and ambitious figure in Zaun, Silco seeks to bring about change through power and manipulation.

Ambessa

A powerful leader in Zaun, known for her ruthlessness and cunning political maneuvers.

Ekko

A brilliant inventor from Zaun, Ekko is known for his time-manipulating device, the Z-Drive.

Singed

A mad scientist and toxicologist, Singed is infamous for his deadly concoctions and experiments.

Mel

A powerful and influential figure in Piltover, known for her political savvy and strategic mind.
